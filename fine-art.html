Fine Art

Although my passion has become digital media, my first love has always been fine art. I love creating my own art as well as immersing myself in other people's work. I hope you enjoy some of my creations below.

My work is mostly a mixture of oil paint and ink. Most recently, my preferred medium has been pen and ink. I have been enjoying stippling and pointilism and am excited to expand my knowledge of the modality.
